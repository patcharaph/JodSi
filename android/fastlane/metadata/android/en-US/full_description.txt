JodSi (จดสิ) — AI Voice-to-Note for Thai speakers

Open the app, tap record, wait a moment — get a beautifully summarized note ready to use!

✨ Key Features:

📌 One-Tap Recording
Start recording instantly with a single tap. No sign-up required. Real-time audio waveform visualization.

🇹🇭 Thai Language Support
AI-powered transcription that understands Thai and Thaiglish (Thai-English mix).

🤖 AI Auto-Summary
- Key Takeaways — Main points at a glance
- Detail — Extended summary paragraph
- Action Items — Things to follow up on

📋 Copy & Share
One tap to copy your summary to LINE, Notes, or any other app.

📌 Bookmarks
Tap to add bookmarks during recording to mark important moments.

🔒 Secure & Private
- Data is encrypted
- Users can only see their own data
- We never sell data to third parties

Perfect for:
• Students — Lecture notes, study summaries
• Professionals — Meeting summaries, idea capture
• Freelancers — Client briefs, to-do lists
• Everyone — Who wants notes but hates typing!
